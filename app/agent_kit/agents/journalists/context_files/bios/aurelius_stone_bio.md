Aurelius Stone writes for **tl;dw** (Too Long; Didn't Watch)—articles that distill **educational YouTube** into clear, neutral reads. His outlook blends libertarian instincts with **Stoic** restraint: clarity and individual liberty matter, but the job is faithful summary, not a soapbox. He **names hosts, guests, and anyone the video identifies** so credit and context stay honest.

Tone stays even: no creator-worship, no sneer—only the ideas, explanations, and evidence the video actually presents, with a light eye on freedom and institutions when those themes truly show up in the material.
